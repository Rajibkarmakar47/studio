# **App Name**: Zero G Hub

## Core Features:

- Navigational Header: A responsive and prominent navigation bar providing links to Home, About Us, Projects, Members, and Contact sections.
- Dynamic Hero Section: An engaging hero section showcasing the 'Team Zero Gravity' brand, mission, and a key call to action.
- About Us Section: A dedicated section to present information about the team, its vision, and values.
- Projects Showcase: A dedicated section to list and display Team Zero Gravity's projects with concise descriptions and relevant visuals.
- Members Showcase: A dedicated section to introduce the individual members of Team Zero Gravity, including their roles, profiles, and possibly gaming specialties or achievements.
- Contact Form: An interactive form allowing visitors to send messages and inquiries directly to the team.
- AI-Powered Information Assistant: An AI tool that leverages the content of the landing page to provide quick answers to user questions.

## Style Guidelines:

- Color scheme: Dark theme, embodying the vastness of space and a sophisticated, tech-focused environment.
- Primary color: A vibrant, clear green (#2EFA4F) that evokes innovation and life, contrasting strongly with the dark background.
- Background color: A very dark, almost black hue with a subtle green tint (#121612), providing a deep canvas for content.
- Accent color: A bright, electric lime green (#B5FE68) to draw attention to interactive elements and highlight key information, complementing the primary green.
- Headline font: 'Space Grotesk' (sans-serif) for a modern, slightly futuristic, and impactful look suitable for the 'Zero Gravity' theme.
- Body font: 'Inter' (sans-serif) for high readability and a clean, professional aesthetic in longer text blocks.
- Use sleek, minimalist line-art icons that maintain a futuristic and clean visual style throughout the site.
- Modern, full-width sections with clear hierarchy. Content is center-aligned for key elements, utilizing ample whitespace to enhance readability against the dark theme.
- Subtle animations on navigation hovers and section transitions to provide a smooth, engaging user experience without distraction.
